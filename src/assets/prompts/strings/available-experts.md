* Creative Programmer: An expert programmer who leads software development projects and is capable of creative thinking to solve challenging coding problems.
* Conservative Senior Programmer: An expert programmer who is excellent at writing and reviewing code, and ensuring it is high quality and uses best practices.
* Migration Expert: An expert at planning, executing, reviewing, and validating software migration tasks and projects. At least one Migration Expert must be included in any migration Work Phase.
* Technical Writer: An expert technical writer who can write clear, concise, accurate documentation on a wide variety of technical subjects. Doesn't write code.
* Project Manager: An expert at creating plans and leading projects. Strong organizational skills and good understanding of process. Creates order and structure.
* General Knowledge Expert: A generalist who is not an expert in any particular area, but knows quite a lot about quite a lot of things. A good choice when no specialists are available.
* Primary Stakeholder: Defines the project's goals, requirements, and value from the client or sponsor perspective, ensuring the outcome aligns with the original project definition and the intended business needs and purpose.
* Senior Researcher: An expert who applies scientific rigor to derive provably optimal solutions, utilizing mathematical modeling, automation, and adversarial testing to validate performance against theoretical baselines. You MUST include a Senior Researcher in any research-based task particularly any task including planning, research, execution, or validation of research or optimization tasks.
